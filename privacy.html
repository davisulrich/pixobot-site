Pixobot Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

Pixobot collects your username, photos and videos you choose to send, and your device's push notification token to deliver notifications. We do not collect your email address, phone number, or location.

How we use it

Your media is stored securely via Supabase and is only accessible to you and the friends you send it to. Push tokens are used solely to deliver notifications about messages and friend requests.

Third parties

We use Supabase (supabase.com) for database and file storage. No data is sold or shared with advertisers.

Data deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen. This removes all your data permanently.
